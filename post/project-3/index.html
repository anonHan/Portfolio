Project-3: American Bank Analysis

By Sahil

July 21, 2022

Introduction:

The banking industry heavily relies on big data analytics. Where real-time analysis of the data helps banks to identify fraudulent transactions, and big data analysis to identify customer spending behavior and loan defaulters.

In this project I worked on a sample of the banking data, which had information about the customers of the American Bank.

Objective:

The aim of this project was to answer the question asked by the bank manager and other stakeholders, such as:

  • How many customers are there from each location?
  • What is their qualification, marital status and gender?
  • How much balance each age group holds in their bank accounts?
  • Is there any relationship between Age and the amount of balance?
  • How to identify a loan defaulter in the future, in order to avoid the risk of fraud?
